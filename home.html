Imagizing Kanye's Quotes!

This website will allow you to generate random quotes from Kanye West. You can then turn each word from the quote into a Wikipedia search. If Wikipedia has an article with the exact title as the quote, you will be able to see an image of that word. The site also keeps track of how many words from Kanye are "Imagiazable", that is, how many words have a Wikipedia article for them. This ratio updates for each word. Enjoy!
